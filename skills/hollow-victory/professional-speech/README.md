## What Problem Does This Solve?

Preparing a team strategy session or client pitch takes hours of structuring and re-structuring. This skill applies consulting storytelling frameworks (Pyramid Principle, SCQA, Minto) to rapidly transform unstructured ideas into clear, persuasive communications with proper agenda, talking points, and discussion guides.
